Design

The main frame is a divided into three areas: top, content and right side area.

  • Top: includes Header, navigation bar and the website title
  • Content: The main content of the site, including text, graphics or animations.
  • Right side: could be used to show additional information or explanation.
  • Footer: powered and copyright information
This style of frame is easy to provide informations about each aspect. The whole color scheme I choose is a gray dark style which seems quiet and comfortable to eyes. Text font and color for the content is adjusted in CSS to give a proper size for navigation.
